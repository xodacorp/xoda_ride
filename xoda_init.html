1. Game Overview & Core Loop

This section breaks down the foundational gameplay experience. XODARIDE relies on a rapid 2-3 minute session loop where players balance risk and reward. Click through the phases below to understand the sequence of a single game session and how players interact with the world.

Target Audience

Mobile gamers (12-35 yrs)

Core Mechanic

Risk-based scoring & evasion

Penalty System

The License Ban

Getting arrested results in a temporary ban, requiring time, ads, or gems to renew.

Phase 1: Preparation & Acquisition

The player spawns into the stylized low-poly city on foot with standard 8-directional movement. To begin the core action, they must acquire a vehicle.

  • Purchase a vehicle legally using accumulated coins/gems.
  • Steal a vehicle (Food cart, Cycle, Bike, Car, or Police Vehicle).
  • Vehicle choice impacts Speed, Handling, Durability, and Acceleration.

Phase 2: The Hook (Infraction)

Players engage in free-roam driving. The environment includes active traffic, pedestrians, and infrastructure. Committing crimes draws police attention.

  • Overspeeding (+1 point per second).
  • Near misses with active traffic (+10 points).
  • Theft triggers immediate points (up to +30 for cop cars).

Phase 3: The Pursuit

Once wanted, high-speed evasion begins. Players must utilize advanced mechanics and map knowledge to survive.

  • Drifting: Simultaneous brake + steer for sharp corners.
  • Nitro: 0.5x speed multiplier boost.
  • Map Utilization: Use highways for speed, tunnels/drainage to break line of sight, and alleyways to shake units.

Phase 4: Conclusion

The session ends when the player successfully evades capture or is apprehended by the police AI.

SUCCESS: Escape

Lose line of sight for 15s. Rewards are multiplied by Wanted Star level.

FAILURE: Arrest

Player receives a License Ban, locking them out of gameplay temporarily.

4. Technical & World Design Framework

Specifications for the environment aesthetics, audio design, and technical performance targets to ensure a smooth, engaging experience on mobile platforms.

🎮

Map Design Features

  • High-Speed: Highways, Bridges
  • Evasion: Tunnels, Drainage, Alleyways
  • Infrastructure: Traffic lights, roundabouts, lane markings, pedestrian zones.
🎨

Aesthetics & Audio

  • Art Style: Stylized low-poly city.
  • Colors: Bright colors, clear road markings, visible police lights.
  • Audio: Dynamic engine/collision sounds. Music intensity (BPM) scales with Wanted Level.
⚙️

Technical Targets

  • Framerate: Constant 60 FPS.
  • Storage: Less than 200MB build size.
  • Memory: Less than 2GB RAM usage for broad Android compatibility.
